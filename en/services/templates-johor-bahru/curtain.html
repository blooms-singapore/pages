Customers must check if the curtain conditions are suitable for machine washing, especially the thin inner linings. They can become brittle due to prolong exposure to sunlight.

Blooms does not markup on the curtain clean. We only provide Picking Service. As such, if there is discrepency, Customers can seek the the laundry shop for clarification.
