Service Info
Standard Installation

Install any items (< 5kg) on walls/ceilings/floors. Service extension will be charged at 30-min blocks.

Standard Installation does not involve water, electrical, human or structural safety. Else, please select Technical Installation.

Minimum 1 Hour booking.

Examples:
  • Wall Paintings.
  • Hooks.
  • Battery Clocks.
  • Clothes Hangers.
  • Key Holders.
  • Bathroom Shelves.
  • Toilet Roll Holders.
  • Dismantle or install curtain (< 3m) etc.
Technical Installation
Installation involving:
  • Electricity.
  • Water or fluid.
  • Direct human safety.
  • Structural safety.
  • Items at heights (> 3m).
  • Heavy items (> 8kg).
  • Glass & Mirror.
  • Assembly tasks.
  • Signal, Radio Frequency, Internet, LAN cabling .
Examples:
  • Wall or Ceiling Fans.
  • Water Heaters.
  • Lights, wall-mounted TV, IKEA shelves.
  • Door Bells.
  • High curtain systems.
  • Bulb Change.
  • Install Power switches etc.
