Service Info
For hygiene considerations, Blooms standard booking will be to use Customers' tools & cleansing solutions.

Should customer need Blooms to bring tools, please select correct option and there will be an additional charge of MYR30.
Standard Cleaning

Scope excludes:

  • lightings, curtains, window blinds, carpets, etc.

  • Any permanent stains.

  • Laundry such as washing socks, towels, floor mats etc.

  • Provision of ladder. Though if customer can provide the ladder, our crews can execute cleaning scope with Customer's instructions or approval.

  • Ironing or laundry (our cleaners are not trained). Clothing can be delicate, expensive & personal. Untrained crews may spoil them.

  • Cleaning at heights over 3m, or any locations which are unsafe.

  • Cleaning or moving of valuable items, major shifting of furniture etc.

  • Cleaning of electrical appliances (Festive Cleaning Scope).

  • Clearing of dead animals, bugs or birds' droppings. (Pest Control Scope).

  • Car Wash.

Festive Cleaning

Low cleaning frequency of once every 2 months or longer is a rule of thumb to enagage Festive Cleaning.

Customers requiring a thorough house clean, involving shift of heavy furniture, requirement to clean under the beds, shelve compartments, light fixtures, insides of closets, cupboards, full window clean etc, in preparation for a festive or an occasion.

Scope excludes:

  • Discarding of rubbish offsite.

  • Provision of carton boxes.

  • Cleaning of Washing Machine.

  • Removal of furniture.

  • Dismantling of items.

  • Cleaning at heights over 3m, or any locations which are unsafe.

  • Upholstery & Curtain cleaning. Our crews are not trained in linen, cotton, polyester etc cleaning.

  • Car Wash.

FAQs

When customer confirms an appointment, but is not home or cancels urgently on the Service day, it is a No-Show event.

There will be a pro-rated charge, to defray our transport & crews' costs, because we cannot immediately seek alternative work for them.

We urge customers to inform about their cancellation as early as possible, so that we can release their reserved timeslot to others who need it.

Service date which is more than 3 days into the future, use the Cancel button at the bottom of Order Confirmation page.

For urgent cancellation, where your Service date is within the next 3 days, please call our Customer Care to inform. There is no cancellation charge.
