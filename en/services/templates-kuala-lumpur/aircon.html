SERVICE INFO

For scope such as aircon repair, relocation &/or installation, a Quote will be provided after a visit.

Note the difference between Survey (free) & Trouble-Shooting (chargeable) services. Refer below FAQ6 for more information.

Standard Wash Scope

  • Check condition of aircon units. Report to customer before proceeding with wash.

  • If a chokage or leak is detected, and issue is severe, a remedy & lumpsum Quote will be provided.

  • Brush & clean blower blades.

  • Clean fancoil front cover, air filters & water tray (Procedures will differ for different AC models).

  • Check coolant gas pressure, electrical cable condition & running current are all optional.

Chemical Overhaul

Thorough wash of internal parts of AC fancoil units. Requires the dismantling of the unit from the wall, components taken apart.

It excludes outdoor compressors.

Stronger chemcials are used to dissolved the slime and kill potential dust mite in the aluminium exchangers.

OTHER CHARGES
Gas Top Up

There are 3 types of refrigerant coolant gases and charges are as follows:

  • R22 @ from RM50 to 80/unit.
  • R32 & R410 @ RM80 to 200/unit.
Customers are unable to choose the type of coolant gas. It is system-dependent.

AFTER SERVICE COMMITMENT
Water Leak or Faults

If your Order is to solve only Aircon water leak or any fault, Blooms charges a minimum 1 hour service, and our crew can work up to 2- 3 units, if time permits.

Should your Order is not for water leak or fault issues, but purely washes, please take note of following:
  • Customer must check and inform Blooms office of any issues within 14 working days of Service date. After this period, if leak or fault occur, it will be treated as a new job request.

  • If the original Service Order is for leak or fault related repairs, Blooms provides 3 months of warranty.

  • Should leak or fault arise from infrastructual defects, inherent from the property, Blooms may choose to not proceed with Service.

Smell & Noise

  • Blooms crews will check for potential noise and odour issues before starting servicing. Customers will be informed of the existing problem(s).

  • Washes may not eliminate odour.
