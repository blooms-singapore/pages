Service Info

Standard Installation

Install any items (< 5kg) on walls, ceilings or floors.

Standard Installation does not involve water, electrical, human or structural safety. Else, please select Technical Installation.

Minimum 1 Hour booking.

Examples:
  • Wall Paintings.
  • Hooks.
  • Battery Clocks.
  • Clothes Hangers.
  • Key Holders.
  • Bathroom Shelves.
  • Toilet Roll Holders.
  • Dismantle or install curtain (< 3m) etc.

Technical Installation

Installations involving:

  • Electricity.
  • Water or fluid.
  • Safety.
  • Items at height.
  • Heavy items.
  • Glass & Mirror.
  • Assembly tasks.
  • Internet, LAN cables.

  • Wall or Ceiling Fans.
