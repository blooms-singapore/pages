Service Info

Install any items (< 5kg) on wall, ceiling or floor. Minimum booking is 0.5 hour.

Examples:
  • Fans, Lights, Water Heaters etc.
  • Wall Paintings and Hooks.
  • Battery Clocks.
  • Clothes Hangers.
  • Key Holders.
  • Toilet Accessories.
  • Dismantle or install curtain (< 3m)
  • DIY Assemblies from IKEA, Shopee, TaoBao etc.
  • Electrical Sockets, Data Points etc.

Technical Installation

Items requiring more skills and care, thus longer service duration, such as:

Installations involving:

  • Electricity.
  • Water or fluid.
  • Safety.
  • Items at height.
  • Heavy items.
  • Glass & Mirror.
  • DIY furniture assemblies.
  • Internet, LAN cables.
