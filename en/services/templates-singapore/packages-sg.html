PACKAGE INFO

Packages purchased during the SPROUT@AMK 3-day event, 12 to 14 July 2024, enjoy an extended card validity of 18 months.

AIRCON STANDARD WASH

  • SGD25 per wall-mount unit.

  • Customers have the choice to purchase a minimum of 9 units and up, base on their requirement/usage. Minimum card price is SGD225 for 9 units.

  • Card will have a unique coupon code tied to the registered address. It is not transferrable, nor refundable.

  • Customers will need to input the coupon code in every booking to enjoy the perks, failing to do so, standard app price applies.

  • Package is for standard wash only. If chemical or other services are required, please refer to app listed prices.

  • SGD80 bonus can be used for all home services in app. Should service fee exceeds SGD80, the shortfall can be paid in Cash, PayNow, Bank TT etc.

  • Customers can only choose 1 discount scheme for the booking. No mutiple discounts can be applied to one booking.

AMK MEMBER DISCOUNT

  • SGD100 per card.

  • Unique coupon code is to be tied to property address, not transferable nor refundable..

  • Customers will need to input the coupon code in every booking to enjoy the perks, failing to do so, standard app price applies.

  • AMK member Discount card has 2 discount schemes, one for app listed prices at 20%, and the other for lumpsum work at 5%.

  • 5% discount for lumpsum work is for the first SGD1,000 only.

  • Customers can only choose 1 discount scheme for the booking. No mutiple discounts can be applied to one booking.

  • Services in app will change with demand and supply. Discounts are also applicable for new service launches.

ONLINE APP PURCHASE

Packages bought on-line via the Blooms app, can still enjoy the extended 18-month card validity, if bookings are received during SPROUT@AMK 12, 13 or 14 July 2024.

APP BOOKING AND CARD RECEIPT

  • Select available Packages.

  • Update accurate address, with postal code, so that the card can be delivered to your property.

  • Only cashless payment is accepted. COD is unavailable.

  • Select any available date as formality to complete the Order. Package card will be posted to you within 7 working days.

  • Should you want to purchase for others, add their addresses and contacts, and make a separate order in their name. But you will need to pay the Order.
