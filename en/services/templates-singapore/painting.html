SERVICE INFO
Time-Charge Service

Budget home painting. Provide us paint, our painters will bring tools, skillfully paint the desired areas for you.

Scope Examples:
  • Small stain on partial wall or all house interiors.

  • Doors, Windows, Shelves.

  • Metal Window Grille, Gate, railings.
Service fee excludes all types of protection materials.

Blooms charges market rate without markup for these extra materials. Customers can specify additional non-painting workscope at our comment window in app, so that our painter comes prepared.

Minimum booking duration for time-charge service is 2.5 hours.

Lumpsum Painting Service

No-hassle home painting. Let us understand your demands, our crews take care of everything else, including the paint.

Work Flow:
  • Book a Survey.

  • Paint crew pays a visit, understand the scope.

  • A competitve Quote will be provided within 3 working days, directly into your App Order.

    Our office will send you a whatsapp notification when the Quote is ready for view.

  • Take time to study the Quote, our crew will follow up with you.

  • When you feel comfortable to proceed, follow the simple booking instructions in the Quote.

Working Time

For Hourly Service booking, please book the earliest time slots in the morning or afternoon. Painting work is unavailable on Sundays and Public holidays.

For Daily Service booking, our crews start at 10am, have an 1+/- hour lunch break and leave the house at 5pm latest, inclusive of cleaning up the site. Work extension beyond 5pm will be considered a time-charge service and hourly rate applies.

For Survey booking, our crew will spend about 1 hour discussing your requirements. We do entertain Survey bookings on Sundays or Public holidays.
