SERVICE INFO
Time-Charge Service

Most budget home painting. Provide us paint, our crews skillfully paint the desired areas for you.

Scope Examples:

  • Small stain on partial wall or all house interiors.

  • Doors, Windows, Shelves.

  • Metal Window Grille, Gate, railings.

Lumpsum Painting Service

No-hassle home painting. Let us understand your demands, our crews take care of everything else, including the paint.

Project Flow:

  • Book a Survey.

  • Paint crew pays a visit, understand the scope.

  • A competitve Quote will be provided within 3 working days, directly into your App Order.

    Our office will send you a whatsapp notification when the Quote is ready for view.

  • Take time to study the Quote, our crew will follow up with you.

  • When you feel comfortable to proceed, follow the simple booking instructions in the Quote.

Working Time

For Hourly Service booking, please book the earliest time slots in the morning or afternoon. Painting work is not available on Sundays and Public holidays.

For Daily Service booking, our crews starts at 10am, have an 1 hour lunch break and leaves the house at about 6pm latest, inclusive of cleaning up the work place.

For Survey booking, our crew will spend about 1 hour discussing your requirements. We do entertain Survey bookings on Sundays.
