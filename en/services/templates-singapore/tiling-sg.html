SERVICE INFO
Time-Charge Service

For micro works which can be completed within 1 day. Area below 80ft2.

All prices listed exclude supply of tiles. Refer FAQ below should customer require Blooms to supply tiles.

Customers can book a videocall or site survey to share their requirements with us.

Please note the following for micro repair service:
  • Crew will bring all necessary tools and materials (sand, cement, plaster etc), excluding the tiles.

  • Service time is calculated according to total time spent in customers' property, including the clean up.

  • Charge is based on actual time used for the entire work, not the duration booked in the Order.

Unit Rate Service

For larger work scope which requires more than 1 day to complete. A site survey is required for site measurement and quote provision.

Please note the following:
  • Official quote can only be provided after a site survey.

  • A price estimate may be possible after a videocall session.
