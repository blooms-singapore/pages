SERVICE INFO
Transport & Trouble-Shoot Fee Waiver

Crew will trouble-shoot the machine fault at site. When it is repair worthy, a solution and quote will be provided, if the repair can be executed on the same day. The price range is listed in the price list.

If the customer agrees to proceed with the work, the Transport & Trouble-shoot fee will be waived.

Quote Provision Information

When the work can be excuted on the same appointment day, a verbal quote will be provided.

If the work scope is complex, lack of time or replacement parts, and cannot be executed on the same appointment day, Blooms will take about 2-5 work days to prepare the Quote. It will be uplaoded to the Order, and customers will ne notified.

In such case, Transport & Trouble-shoot fee is still incurred, but it will be refunded when the customer decides to proceed with the work, in the future.

Booking Tips

  • Payment mode can be in Cash, e-Wallet or Credit Card.

  • Warranty is 1 week. Refer to FAQ for more information.

  • There is no same day service, due to labour crunch. We encourage customers to plan ahead.

  • Crew can also execute plumbing tasks.

  • 1 crew attends to you.

  • There is no "Customer Purchase Parts, Blooms Only Repair" Service. Please refer to below FAQ for more information.
